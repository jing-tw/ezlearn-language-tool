固定至開始功能表  (Pin the EZLearn to the Start Menu)

Automatic setup
	-Install: 
		./EZLearn/01_pin_to_start_menul.bat
	
	-Unstall(remove the EZLearn from the menu):
		./EZLearn/02_unpin_to_start_menul.bat
	 
	
or you can setup by manual as following instructions.

Manual setup
Chinese 
Step 1: 先對 EZLearn.exe 建立捷徑
Step 2: 對該捷徑按右鍵, 選擇 "固定至開始功能表"

English
Step 1: At first, make a shortcut to the EZLearn.exe
Step 2: Click your Right mouse button, and then pin it to your Start Menu.
